WHAT IS THE LANNA INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL SNACK SHOP ALL ABOUT?

Welcome to Lanna International's School very own, student run, snack shop!

The Lanna snack shop offers a wide variety of brain food. Ranging from savory pizza inspired pretzel bread to sweet refreshing fruit drinks. All items are reasonably priced, offering something for everyone.

"Food is just like duck tape, it fixes everything."

Although the snack shop is always changing up their menu and looking for new things to add, here are some of Lanna's most popular snacks among the students...

MENU
Pretzel Buns: Classic preztel bread sprinkled with sea salt. Pizza Buns: Inspired by the italian dish, these pretzel buns are topped with tomato sauce, meat, peppers and cheese. 'Moo-ping' and Sticky Rice: The Thai pork skewer delicacy, also known as 'moo-ping', serevd with traditional sticky rice.
Chocolate Croissants: Traditional French croissants with sweet chocolate stuffed inside and drizzled on top. Brownies: Classic, chewy, chocolate brownies. Cookies: Classic, chewy, chocolate chip cookies.
Rice Crackers: Thai rice crackers with sweet syrup drizzled on the top. Chocolate Wafer Rolls: Crunchy wafer rolls with chocolate lined inside. 'Koala's March': The famous koala shaped bite sized crackers stuffed with chocolate.
'Chicken Legs': Sweet and spicy bread sticks, also known as ขาไก่, which is the literal translation of 'chicken legs'. Yogurt: Refridgerated yogurt ranging in flavors. Juice Boxes: Chilled fruit juice ranging in flavors.
