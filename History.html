WHO RUNS THE LANNA SNACK SHOP?

The Lanna snack shop is run by the Year 10 IGCSE Enterprise students!

The Lanna snack shop has been in business ever since August of 2018 and has been going on ever since. Through running the snack shop, students gain and imrpove a variety of skills including; organization, time-management, communication, team-work and much more.
