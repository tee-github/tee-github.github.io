SIGNATURE RECIPES

All items sold at the snack shop are made with the freshest, highest quality ingredients.

Here are the "top secret" recipes for the three staple items sold at the snack shop: pretzel buns, chocolate croissants, and cookies.

MENU INGREDIENTS
Pretzel Buns
  • 3 3/4 cups plus 1 tablespoon flour (preferably white spelt or unbleached wheat)
  • 1 1/4-ounce envelope fact-acting yeast
  • 2 teaspoons fine sea salt
  • 1 1/4 cups water, lukewarm
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 3 tablespoons baking soda, for the alkaline solution
  • coarse sea salt
